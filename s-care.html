Why Self Care?

Self Care is so important and is way to oftern overlooked and forgotten. Some people may even feel guity taking time for themselves. But Self Care is crucial for personal development. Self Care affects all aspects of our life including Mental, Emotional, and Physical Health. We are more resilent for life's ups and downs when we take care of our body, mind, and soul. Self Care doesn't need to be grand or expensive, it can be as simple as taking a walk to clear you mind and sit in your own thoughts. “Self-care is never a selfish act—it is simply good stewardship of the only gift I have, the gift I was put on earth to offer to others.” – Parker Palmer

What is Self Care?

Self care is defined as any activity that we do intentionally to care for our Mental, Emotional, and Physical Health.

Mental Health

Taking Care of our Self helps us mantain good Mental Health. Ways to Improve your Mental Health by Self Care are by challenging yourself. You can do this by learning wordds in a foreign language, trying a new sport, or even taking a different route to work.

Emotional Health

Emotional Health can take many forms in regard to Self Care. You can reflect on you day and call to mind all you are greatful for and everything that was good that happened. You can also take five minutes to sit in your own thought.

Physical Health

Self Care plays a huge part of Physical Health. Sleep is so important for physical wellbeing. Sleep is the key that sets you up for success. Also eating right moderates the bacteria that lives in your stomach resulting in postive/negative wellbeing.
