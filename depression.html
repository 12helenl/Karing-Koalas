Understanding Depression

About...

There are multiple types of depression. They include persistent depressive disorder, postpartum depression, seasonal affective disorder, and bipolar disorder. Factors that can cause depression include personal or family history of depression, trauma, stress, and certain medications. Syptoms and signs of depression include persistent low mood, sense of despair, and sadness. Physical symptoms can include aches, cramps, headaches, difficulty sleeping and weight changes. See a medical profession for further help and treatment methods.
