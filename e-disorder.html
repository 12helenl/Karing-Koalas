Understanding Eating Disorders

About . . .

There are multiple types of eating disorders. Several factors that can cause eating disorders. Brain biology, personality traits and cultural ideas. Brain biology is how the brain functions. People can have different moods which will affect their personality and their ability to make decisions. Persona traits will relate to perfectonist, they want to match societies ideas of "pretty". Sadly, being skinny is a goal to many people. This gets in their head and causes them to see themselves as overweight.

Types of Eating Disorders

Anorexia

Someone who has anorexia tends to see themselves as overweight, however these people could be underweight compared to others their age. They tend to deny that they are undeweight and ontinue to keep unhealthly not eating.
